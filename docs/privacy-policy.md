# LuaNet privacy policy

Last updated: 29 June 2026

LAN hosting works without an account and sends no hosting data to NovaX. Public tunnelling
requires a verified Firebase account. NovaX stores the account identifier, registered
devices, tunnel leases, aggregate usage, Google Play purchase entitlement, and
security/anti-abuse logs.
Security logs are deleted after 30 days.

LuaNet does not upload worlds, game chat, player names, installed content, or console logs to
NovaX. Public game packets pass through the NovaX relay in transit but are not retained.

Crash reporting is disabled unless the user opts in. LuaNet does not perform behavioural
analytics. The EEA consent flow is shown before AdMob is used. LAN hosting never displays an
advertisement. Google, Firebase, Google Play, AdMob, and ContentDB process data under their
own terms when the corresponding feature is used.

Users can delete their cloud account from the app; this revokes active tunnels and removes
account, device, lease, and entitlement records, except records that must temporarily remain
for security or legal obligations. Local server profiles and worlds stay on the phone until
the user removes the app data.

LuaNet is intended for a Teen audience and is not directed to children. Privacy requests:
`luanet@novaxhosting.com`.
